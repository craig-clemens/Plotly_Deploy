Why Is My Belly Button Smelly?

Mostly, the baceria in belly buttons are harmless, but they can multiply and cause infection. In order to keep ourselves safe from infection (and smelling great!) we need to keep it clean and healthy.

How Often Should I Be Cleaning My Belly Button?

The human belly button is full of different crevices that can collect dirt and breed bacteria. Ideally one should aim to clean theirs about once a week.
